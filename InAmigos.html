Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, and is headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government of India.

We believe in the power of collective action to address society's deepest challenges — mobilizing volunteers, resources, and compassion to bring lasting change to underserved communities across the country.

✔ 80G & 12A Certified ✔ CSR-1 Registered ✔ NITI Aayog Listed ✔ ISO 9001:2015 ✔ NGO Darpan

Our Ongoing Projects

Every project targets a specific gap in society — together, they form a complete ecosystem of care and empowerment.

🍲

Project Sewa

Distributing food and clothing to underprivileged and homeless individuals through regular feeding and relief drives.

📚

Project Bachpanshala

Creating learning spaces and quality education access for children who otherwise have no schooling due to poverty.

🐾

Project Jeev

Animal welfare initiatives including rescue, protection, and feeding drives for stray and injured animals.

🚀

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and financial independence, helping them become confident contributors to society.

🌱

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation through tree plantation drives, contributing to cleaner air and a sustainable tomorrow.

💼

Project Vikas

Skill development and internship programs enhancing employability, having already trained over 30,000 interns.

Our Social Impact
